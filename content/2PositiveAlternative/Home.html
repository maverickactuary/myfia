An alternative actuarial home page

Actuaries provide expert assessments of financial security systems, with a focus on the systems' complexity, mathematics and mechanisms. Actuaries bring particular expertise to the financial impact of risk and uncertainty.

The Institute and Faculty of Actuaries is the UK's professional body for actuaries: modern, but with with a heritage stretching back to 1848.
